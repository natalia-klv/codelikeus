Simple but Sexy

A black dress is always the quickiest simplest choice, but at the same time the safest choice. You can always go for a black dress and if you style it with the appropriate acessories you'll most definitely get an ideal look. To finish up your look long earrings (e.g: hoops) and high heels will certaintly freshen-up your look. Last but not least don't forget to put on your brightest smile!

Colourful but Humble

Prom is a very special event that happens only once in your life, so in order to be crowned queen of the night you have to feel and be dressed like one. Avoid basic colours such as black and white and spice-up your outfit by wearring cute colours like baby blue, baby pink, different shades of red, and dark green. Do not over do it with your makeup and your accesories remember that the dress is enough to show of your beauty.

Comfy but Elegant

If you want an outfit for a daily basis an oversized hoodie or t-shirt can replace your commonly worn dress. Generally, oversized clothes are very practical and can be easily styled. For example, oversized dresses can be worn with high and low boots, snickers (vans,airforce,all star), hats (beannies,caps,bucket hats,berets), big or small bags plus discrete earrings and rings.
